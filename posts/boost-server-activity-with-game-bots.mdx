### What are Game Bots?
Game bots make adding minigames to your Discord server an easy and streamline process. With [thousands of bots to pick from](https://top.gg/tag/gaming), you can find the best pick (or picks) for your community! The best part? Bots remove the need for complex setup, allowing members to enjoy them almost instantly.

### Which Game Bots should I use?
Our very own [Would You](https://wouldyoubot.gg/invite) includes a number of minigames, such as What Would You Do, Higher or Lower, and Truth or Dare. However, there are plenty of other awesome bots you should check out too!
- [Tiny Games](https://tinygames.xyz/) brings classic games to life within your Community. Enjoy over 24 well built games with classics like 2048, Connect 4, and Flood to name a few.
- [Idle Miner](https://top.gg/bot/518759221098053634) combines idle games and Minecraft into one bot. Upgrade your pickaxe and backpack to explore the world of Idle Miner, along with managing your own farm, pets, boosters, and more!
- [Dank Memer](https://dankmemer.lol/commands) is one of the more well known game bots on Discord. With a unique economy system, members are sure to enjoy competing, collecting special items, and climbing their way to the top of the leaderboard.
- [Countr](https://countr.xyz) is perfect for all your counting needs. With a simple setup, Countr allows for a fully automated counting channel. With a leaderboard, spam prevention, and competitive settings, members are sure to enjoy this bot.
